About Jordan Tupper

Baton Rouge musician · bassist · vocalist · music educator

Biography

Jordan Tupper is a Baton Rouge, Louisiana-based musician, bassist, vocalist, music educator, and community leader. He performs throughout Louisiana with Night Hog, Cover 6 Band, and Standley, Tupper & Petersen.

Jordan’s work connects live performance, music education, church music, event production, and community engagement. As a performer, he brings a practical, room-focused approach to the stage: steady bass, strong vocals, reliable preparation, and a commitment to making live music feel good for the audience and the venue.

As an educator and program leader, Jordan has more than 19 years of experience building music programs, organizing ensembles, directing performances, and supporting students and communities through music. He was named a national quarterfinalist for the 2024 Grammy Music Educator Award.

Background

Jordan is a graduate of Elon University and Austin Peay State University and holds professional certifications in digital marketing and communications, including HubSpot certifications and an LSU Digital Marketing Certificate. He has used that experience to support music promotion, nonprofit work, community events, and local creative projects.

He is active in the Baton Rouge community and in the worship life of First Presbyterian Church Baton Rouge.
