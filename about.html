Biography

Who Is Jordan Tupper?

Jordan Tupper is a Baton Rouge, Louisiana–based musician, music educator, bandleader, and community leader whose career spans more than 19 years across live performance, music education, program management, and nonprofit leadership. Known for his versatility both on stage and in the community, Jordan Tupper brings a rare combination of artistic depth, organizational expertise, and genuine passion for service to everything he does.

Born with a deep love for music, Jordan Tupper built his professional foundation in music education before expanding into community engagement, leadership, and the performing arts. Today he is one of Baton Rouge's most active working musicians, performing regularly with three distinct musical groups while staying deeply rooted in the faith and civic life of the Greater Baton Rouge community.

Grammy Music Educator Award — National Quarterfinalist
Jordan Tupper was recognized as a national quarterfinalist for the Grammy Music Educator Award, one of the most prestigious honors in music education in the United States. This recognition reflects his lasting impact on students and communities throughout his career.

Career

Career & Background

Jordan Tupper's professional journey began in music education, where he served as a band director and music teacher across multiple schools in Middle Tennessee and Louisiana. His work building award-winning programs from the ground up earned him recognition as one of the region's most dedicated and effective music educators.

Throughout his career, Jordan Tupper has demonstrated a consistent ability to lead teams, manage complex programs, and build meaningful relationships with students, parents, and community stakeholders. These same skills have carried over into his work as a bandleader, entrepreneur, and community volunteer.

Jordan Tupper also holds an LSU Digital Marketing Certificate from Louisiana State University, reflecting his commitment to continuing education and his ability to apply modern marketing strategies to music promotion, brand building, and community engagement efforts.

Faith & Community
