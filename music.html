Overview

Jordan Tupper, Baton Rouge Musician

Jordan Tupper is one of Baton Rouge's most active working musicians, performing regularly across the city and surrounding region with three distinct musical groups. From high-energy classic rock to sophisticated jazz standards, Jordan Tupper brings versatility, professionalism, and 19+ years of musical experience to every performance.

Whether you're looking to book a band for a corporate event, a private party, a wedding, or a venue night, Jordan Tupper and his ensembles deliver memorable live music experiences tailored to your audience and occasion.

Grammy Music Educator Award — National Quarterfinalist
Jordan Tupper's musical credibility runs deep. He was recognized as a national quarterfinalist for the Grammy Music Educator Award — a testament to the artistry and dedication he brings to music both as a performer and an educator.
