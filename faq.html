FAQ

Common questions about Jordan Tupper — music, background, and bookings

Who is Jordan Tupper?

Jordan Tupper is a Baton Rouge, Louisiana-based musician, music educator, and community leader with more than 19 years of experience in live performance, band direction, program management, and community engagement.

What bands does Jordan Tupper perform with?

Jordan Tupper performs bass and vocals with three active groups: Night Hog (classic rock, funk, and soul), Cover 6 Band (a versatile live cover band), and Jim Standley Jazz (a Baton Rouge jazz ensemble).

Is Jordan Tupper a music educator?

Yes. Jordan Tupper was named a national quarterfinalist for the 2024 Grammy Music Educator Award by the Recording Academy and GRAMMY Museum, selected from more than 2,000 nominees nationwide. He has taught music at Episcopal School of Baton Rouge and previously served as a band director in Cheatham County, Tennessee.

Where is Jordan Tupper based?

Jordan Tupper lives and works in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, performing throughout the Greater Baton Rouge area and surrounding Louisiana and Mississippi region.

What is Jordan Tupper's professional background?

Beyond music, Jordan Tupper has a background in program management, team leadership, and digital marketing, holding an LSU Digital Marketing Certificate and HubSpot certifications in Social Media Marketing and Email Marketing. He is a graduate of Elon University and Austin Peay State University.

How can I book Jordan Tupper or one of his bands?

Each band has its own booking contact: visit nighthogbr.com for Night Hog, cover6band.com for Cover 6 Band, or batonrougejazz.com for Jim Standley Jazz.

Where can I watch Jordan Tupper perform?

A full library of live performance videos is available on the Videos page and on the @jordanjtupper YouTube channel.

Does Jordan Tupper do anything outside of music?

Yes — Jordan Tupper is a professional travel agent with TupperTravel and co-founder of Pin & Post Co., a custom bulletin board company based in Baton Rouge.

What is Jordan Tupper's connection to First Presbyterian Church Baton Rouge?

Jordan Tupper is an active member of First Presbyterian Church Baton Rouge and is involved in the civic and faith life of the Greater Baton Rouge community.

Where can I find more about Jordan Tupper's music career specifically?

Visit JordanTupperMusic.com for performance details and band information, or About.JordanTupperMusic.com for an extended music biography.
